### Tactics (1000pt)

- **Treelord Ancient** is your General and only Wizard. Use Lore of the Deepwood spells and his support ability to buff Kurnoth Hunters (+1 to hit in melee on a 3+)
- **Kurnoth Hunters (Greatbows)** snipe enemy heroes from 18" -- they ignore Guarded Hero, which is rare and powerful. Screen them with Dryads
- **Revenant Seekers** are fast cavalry (12" move + FLY) -- grab flanking objectives and harass. With Outcasts, enemy units in combat with them lose -3 Control Score
- **Branchwych** leads a Dryad unit, providing +1 to wound in melee for the regiment
- **Dryads** are your cheap screens and objective holders. Keep them near Awakened Wyldwood for -1 to hit and wound (Blessings of the Forest)
- **Awakened Wyldwood** deploys free in your territory. It gives your units cover, blocks line of sight for small units, and deals D3 mortal wounds to nearby enemies on a 4+

### Tactics (2000pt)

- **The Lady of Vines** is your key support piece. Her Rampage either gives all enemy attackers targeting her -1 to hit, or lets all friendly Sylvaneth get +1 to hit against a chosen target. Use the +1 to hit mode when you have a priority kill target
- **Kurnoth Hunters (Greatbows)** continue to snipe heroes. At 2000pts opponents bring more heroes, making these even more valuable
- **Kurnoth Hunters (Greatswords)** are your dedicated melee unit. Pair with Lady of Vines' +1 to hit buff for devastating charges
- **Drycha Hamadreth** is a damage-dealing monster who can unleash either a swarm of Flitterfuries (anti-horde) or Squirmlings (anti-elite). Pick based on your opponent's army
- **Spiterider Lancers** hit hard on the charge (220pts reflects their damage output). Use them as a flanking hammer alongside Revenant Seekers
- **Revenant Seekers** (180pts) are your fast objective-grabbing cavalry. With Outcasts' -3 Control Score, a single unit of Seekers can contest objectives held by much larger enemy units
- **Gossamid Archers** provide ranged chip damage and have FLY for mobility. Use them to hold objectives on terrain pieces
- **Awakened Wyldwood** is even more important at 2000pts -- deploy it to protect your backline objective. Enemy units inside take D3 mortal wounds on a 4+, and your units nearby get cover

## Awakened Wyldwood -- Matched Play Notes

The Awakened Wyldwood is Sylvaneth's **faction terrain** and costs 0 points. Key rules:
- Deploy 1-3 scenery pieces wholly within your territory, 3"+ from objectives and other terrain
- **Vengeful Forest Spirits:** Roll a dice for each enemy unit in combat range; on 4+, deal D3 mortal wounds
- **Cover:** Units behind/on the Wyldwood get -1 to hit from ranged attacks
- **Line of Sight:** Non-MONSTER, non-FLY infantry within 1" are only visible to units in combat range
- **Blessings of the Forest:** Friendly Sylvaneth units wholly within 6" get -1 to enemy hit AND wound rolls (faction ability)
- Many Sylvaneth abilities (teleportation via Walk the Hidden Paths, healing, summoning) key off proximity to Awakened Wyldwoods
- **You own 1 Wyldwood kit** -- this is enough. Belthanos can create a second from an existing terrain piece during the game
