### Painting Priority (2000pt additions)
1. **Lychguard x10** — core melee brick, pairs with Translocation Overlord
2. **Immortals x10** — reliable objective holders, batch paint like Warriors
3. **Canoptek Wraiths x6** — fast attack, batch in pairs of 3
4. **C'tan Shard of the Void Dragon** — centerpiece, take your time
5. **Triarch Stalker** — repair assembly, then Warrior scheme (single model)
6. **Psychomancer + Overlord #2** — character models, quick

### Tactics at 2000pt
- **Lychguard + Overlord w/ Translocation Shroud** is your hammer — deep strike 10 Lychguard with 4++ invuln into the enemy's lines
- **C'tan Shard of the Void Dragon** is nearly unkillable (max 3 wounds per phase) and deletes vehicles — force your opponent to split fire
- **Canoptek Wraiths** are your fastest unit (10" move, Wraith-form 4++ invuln) — use them to grab flanking objectives or wrap enemy units in combat
- **Immortals** hold mid-board objectives while Warriors + Characters form a Reanimation castle in the back
- **Triarch Stalker** marks a target — any other Necron unit shooting that target gets +1 to hit. Fire it first, then unload Warriors/Immortals
- **Psychomancer** debuffs enemy units with fear effects — attach to a Warrior block or use standalone to reduce enemy effectiveness
