# Stormcast Eternals Project Plan — Tempest of Souls

> Points from Wahapedia AoS 4th Edition / Battletome Supplement (current as of April 2026)

---

## IMPORTANT: Legends Status

**Every Stormcast Eternals unit from Tempest of Souls moved to Warhammer Legends on 1 June 2025.** This means none of these models are legal in matched play or competitive events under current AoS 4th edition rules. They remain usable in:
- Casual / open play games (with opponent's agreement)
- Narrative / Crusade campaigns
- Spearhead (if a Spearhead warscroll exists)
- Painting projects (obviously)

This is not unusual for a 2018 starter set — GW has cycled out the entire Sacrosanct Chamber. If you want a competitive Stormcast force, you would need to start fresh with current-range models (Thunderstrike / Ruination chambers).

### Critical Problem: Evocators

You only have 2 Evocators from Tempest of Souls, but the minimum unit size is 5. This unit cannot be fielded without buying 3 more Evocators — and since the kit is discontinued, you would need to source them secondhand. Given their Legends status, this is not worth the effort for gaming purposes. They remain excellent painting projects.

## 500pt Army List (Legends / Casual Only)

This list uses Legends warscrolls and requires your opponent's agreement.

| Unit | Models | Pts | Notes |
|------|--------|-----|-------|
| Knight-Incantor | 1 | 140 | General, Wizard |
| Sequitors | 5 | 120 | Battleline equivalent |
| Castigators | 3 | 110 | Ranged support |
| Celestar Ballista | 1 | 120 | War Machine |
| **Total** | **10** | **490** | |

The Evocators cannot be included (only 2 models, minimum is 5).

### Painting List
1. **Knight-Incantor** (1 model) — centrepiece hero, paint first for motivation
2. **Sequitors x5** (5 models) — batch paint, core infantry
3. **Castigators x3** (3 models) — batch paint with Sequitors (similar armour)
4. **Celestar Ballista** (1 model + 2 crew) — paint last, fun detail piece
5. **Evocators x2** (2 models) — paint as display pieces / practice models

### Casual Tactics
- Knight-Incantor provides magic support and spell denial with Voidstorm Scroll (once per battle unbind)
- Sequitors hold objectives as your main body
- Castigators and Celestar Ballista provide ranged firepower
- This is a very small, static force — it will struggle against most 500pt armies but works fine for casual intro games

## 1000pt Army List — NOT RECOMMENDED

Building to 1000pt with Legends Stormcast requires significant investment in discontinued models. This is not advisable. If you want a 1000pt Stormcast army, consider:

### Option A: Start Fresh (Current Range)
Buy a Stormcast Eternals Spearhead box and build from there with current, supported units:

| Unit | Pts | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| Yndrasta | 340 | Included in Spearhead box |
| Knight-Vexillor | 120 | Included in Spearhead box |
| Vanquishers x10 | 110 | Included in Spearhead box |
| Annihilators x3 | 200 | Included in Spearhead box |
| Liberators x5 | 90 | Buy separately |
| Lord-Relictor | 120 | Buy separately |
| **Total** | **980** | |

### Option B: Treat as Paint-Only Project
Paint the Tempest of Souls Stormcast as a self-contained painting project. They are beautiful Sacrosanct Chamber sculpts — the Knight-Incantor in particular is a fantastic model. No need to expand for gaming.

**Recommendation: Option B.** These are tertiary models from a starter box. Paint them, enjoy them, and invest gaming energy in Sylvaneth (your main AoS army) or Nighthaunt (the other half of this box, which IS still playable).

## Paint Scheme Suggestions

### Classic Hammers of Sigmar (Gold & Blue)
The "default" Stormcast scheme and arguably the most striking:
- **Armour**: Retributor Armour base, Reikland Fleshshade wash, Liberator Gold layer, Stormhost Silver edge highlights
- **Cloth/Tabard**: Kantor Blue base, Drakenhof Nightshade wash, Caledor Sky layer
- **Weapons**: Leadbelcher base, Nuln Oil wash, Ironbreaker highlight
- **Skin/Leather**: Rhinox Hide base, Doombull Brown layer

### Celestial Vindicators (Turquoise & Gold)
A more unique scheme that contrasts well with the gold:
- **Armour**: Retributor Armour base, Agrax Earthshade wash
- **Cloth**: Thousand Sons Blue, shaded with Coelia Greenshade
- **Details**: Same metallics as above

### Hallowed Knights (Silver & Blue)
A cleaner, brighter look:
- **Armour**: Leadbelcher base, Nuln Oil wash, Stormhost Silver highlight
- **Cloth**: Macragge Blue, Altdorf Guard Blue layer

### Painting Resources
- **Warhammer TV** has multiple tutorials for Stormcast (search "Stormcast Eternals" on YouTube)
- **Duncan Rhodes Painting Academy** did Sequitors and Evocators in the classic gold scheme
- The Tempest of Souls booklet itself includes a painting guide for these exact models
