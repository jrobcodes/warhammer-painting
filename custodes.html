Recommended Painting Order

1
Spray gold all Custodians (Shield-Captain, Guard, Praetors). Spray black all Prosecutors.
2
Shade gold with Reikland Fleshshade Gloss — do all gold models at once. While drying, basecoat black joints on Custodians.
3
Basecoat reds (Mephiston Red) on all cloth, capes, plumes. 2 thin coats.
4
Layer gold (Liberator Gold) on raised surfaces. Shade red (Carroburg Crimson) in folds.
5
Highlight gold edges (Stormhost Silver). Layer red (Evil Sunz Scarlet) on raised folds.
6
Prosecutors: Grey drybrush on armor, Leadbelcher on trim, Nuln Oil wash, robes, then skin last.
7
Base all models together at the end. Keep it simple — texture paint + drybrush + tufts.
