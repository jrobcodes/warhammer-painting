### Additional paints vs Warrior scheme
- Retributor Armour — gold details on Overlord
- Wyldwood — Contrast for robes/cloth
- Liberator Gold — gold highlight
- Ironhands Steel — darker steel
- Basilicanum Grey — Contrast for certain panels
- Cryptek Armourshade Gloss — glossy metallic wash
- Corax White — white base
- Administratum Grey — grey highlight
