## Common Sylvaneth Palette (shared across tutorials)
These paints appear in multiple Sylvaneth schemes above:
- **Bark/wood:** Dryad Bark, Gorthor Brown, Baneblade Brown
- **Green foliage:** Deathworld Forest, Athonian Camoshade, Elysian Green, Nurgling Green, Caliban Green, Warpstone Glow, Moot Green
- **Bone/spirit:** Rakarth Flesh, Ushabti Bone, Seraphim Sepia
- **Leather/straps:** XV-88, Agrax Earthshade, Tau Light Ochre
- **Blue spirit glow:** Sotek Green, Temple Guard Blue, Baharroth Blue, Drakenhof Nightshade
