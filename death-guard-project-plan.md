## Paint Scheme Suggestions

### Recommended: Classic Death Guard Green (Beginner-Friendly)
Death Guard are one of the most forgiving armies to paint. The grimy, corroded aesthetic means mistakes look intentional, and heavy washes do most of the work.

**Speed-paint method:**
1. Prime with **Death Guard Green Spray** (Citadel spray -- base + primer in one)
2. Wash entire model liberally with **Agrax Earthshade** (creates instant grime in recesses)
3. Drybrush raised armor with **Death Guard Green** to restore color
4. Light drybrush with **Ogryn Camo** for edge highlights
5. Trim and details: **Balthasar Gold** + **Nihilakh Oxide** for verdigris on bronze
6. Tentacles/flesh: **Rakarth Flesh** + **Druchii Violet** wash + **Pallid Wych Flesh** highlight
7. Rust effects: **Typhus Corrosion** + **Ryza Rust** drybrush (technical paints)
8. Slime/ooze: **Nurgle's Rot** technical paint in wounds and joints

**Poxwalker speed method:**
1. Prime **Wraithbone** spray
2. Apply various Contrast paints per area (skin, cloth, bone) -- no need for consistency, they are shambling zombies
3. Wash everything with **Agrax Earthshade**
4. Done. Poxwalkers should look rough.
