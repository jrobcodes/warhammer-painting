Lychguard Scheme Note: Lychguard use a heavier gold treatment than standard characters. Base gold areas with Retributor Armour, then highlight with Liberator Gold. Apply generously across armor plates, shoulders, and shields (not just trim). Wash gold with Cryptek Armourshade Gloss for a rich, warm finish. The rest of the model follows the standard Warrior metallic scheme.
