Special Models — Quick Notes

Lychguard: Warrior scheme for body (Zones A-E). Then Zone F gold treatment but MUCH more — cover entire shield face, full shoulder plates, chest plate, helmet. Way more gold than Overlord. Wash with Cryptek Armourshade Gloss.
Scarab Swarms (5 min speed paint): Prime black, heavy drybrush Leadbelcher, Nuln Oil wash, Stormhost Silver on a few wing edges, Moot Green dot on eyes. Done.
Canoptek Wraiths: Warrior scheme for body. Tail/whip = dark metallic. Silver edge highlights on claw tips. Green glow on head sensor.
C'tan Void Dragon: Dark metallic body (Zone A). Skip bronze trim — this isn't a Necron soldier. Instead, the energy body gets the FULL green glow treatment across all tendrils, chest core, and base effects. This is a showpiece — take your time.
