Dark Metallic Body (steps 1-3)
5,7
Leadbelcher Spray → Black Templar → Nuln Oil
Entire body, gun housing, arms, legs, torso. This is the base — everything starts metallic, then gets darkened with Contrast + wash. Other colors go on top.
Bronze/Brass Trim (steps 4-6)
3,4,9
Runelord Brass → Canoptek Alloy → Cryptek Armourshade
Shoulder pads, kneecaps, chest Szarekhan symbol, spine ridge. All the raised decorative trim and dynasty markings. Highlight upper edges with Canoptek Alloy, wash recesses with Armourshade.
Silver Edge Highlights (step 7)
1,10
Stormhost Silver
Sharpest edges only: head crest tips, ribcage plate edges, leg armor ridges, arm plate edges. Dots on rivets. Thin lines — less is more.
Green Gauss Glow (steps 8-15)
2,6,8
Black → Caliban Green → Warpstone Glow → Moot Green → White dot
Eyes (small ovals on face), gauss flayer tube (curved tube under gun — green stripe), gun vents (4 circular ports on gun barrel). Paint inside-out: dark edges → bright center.
Basing (step 16)
11
Moon Dust
Base rim + texture. Desert/tomb world theme.
Paint order: Spray Leadbelcher → Black Templar all over → Nuln Oil in recesses → Bronze trim → Bronze wash → Silver edges → Black out glow areas → Green glow layers → Biel-tan Green glaze → Base

Polish Wash

Cryptek Armourshade Gloss
Over all gold areas + blade. Thin coat — gives a rich, polished sheen. Don't let it pool on flat gold.
Gold hierarchy: Warriors = no gold. Overlord = gold face, chest, trim. Lychguard = gold everywhere (shields, full shoulders, chest plates).

C'tan Void Dragon Entire energy body — tendrils, chest core, eyes, base lightning. Go heavy on glow.
Lychguard: Warrior metallic base, then heavy gold (Retributor Armour + Liberator Gold) across entire shield face, full shoulder plates, chest plate, helmet. Much more gold than Overlord — these are elite bodyguards. Wash gold with Cryptek Armourshade Gloss.
Scarab Swarms (speed method): Prime black → heavy drybrush Leadbelcher → Nuln Oil wash → few wing edges with Stormhost Silver → eyes with Moot Green dot. 5 minutes per base.
Canoptek Wraiths: Warrior scheme for the body. The tail/whip is dark metallic. Green glow on the head sensor and any visible energy nodes. Silver edge highlights on the sharp claw tips.
