Key principle: More gold = higher rank. Warriors get no gold. Overlord gets gold face, chest, trim. Lychguard get gold everywhere (shields, full shoulders, chest plates). The brass trim (Runelord Brass) is the "standard" metallic accent; gold (Retributor) is reserved for status.
Green glow areas on Overlord: Same as Warrior — eyes, orb on weapon/staff, any gauss elements. Follow Warrior steps 8-15 for these areas.

Where this glow goes on the model:
  • Eyes — small ovals on the face, do the full gradient
  • Gauss rod (Warriors) — the long rod on the gun. Paint as a stripe: black edges → green center → bright line down the middle
  • Chest orb — small circle on the torso, full gradient
  • Gun barrel interior — dark at the edges, green glow inside
  • Resurrection orb (Overlord) — large sphere on the staff, full gradient
  • Gauss blaster coils (Immortals) — tubes along the gun barrel
  • OSL halo (optional) — thin wash of Moot Green + Mechanicus Std Grey mixed on the metal around glow sources
