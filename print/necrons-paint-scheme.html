Overlord scheme: Overlord(s), Skorpekh Lord, Plasmancer, Technomancer, Psychomancer
Special: C'tan Shard of the Void Dragon (custom — green energy + dark metallic body)

Lychguard — Extra Gold Treatment

Use the Warrior metallic base, then apply heavier gold coverage with Retributor Armour (base) and Liberator Gold (layer) across the shield faces, shoulder plates, and chest. These are elite bodyguards — more gold = higher rank.
