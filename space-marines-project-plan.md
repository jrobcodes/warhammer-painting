## Paint Scheme Suggestions

### Recommended: Ultramarines (Classic & Beginner-Friendly)
The Dark Imperium and Indomitus models were designed as Ultramarines, so transfers are included in both boxes. This is the easiest path.

**Speed-paint method:**
1. Prime with **Macragge Blue Spray** (base + primer in one step)
2. Recess shade with **Nuln Oil**
3. Layer raised areas with **Macragge Blue** (touch-up)
4. Edge highlight with **Calgar Blue**, then fine highlights with **Fenrisian Grey**
5. Gold trim: **Retributor Armour** + **Reikland Fleshshade**
6. Apply Ultramarines transfers from box set sheets

**Alternative fast method:** Prime **Grey Seer**, apply **Talassar Blue** Contrast over everything for a quick, effective blue.

### Alternative Chapters
If you want something different from Ultramarines, these are good options that use the same Space Marines codex rules:
- **Salamanders** -- green armor, fire effects, forgiving to paint (dark green hides mistakes)
- **Imperial Fists** -- yellow is hard to paint but striking on the table
- **Blood Angels** -- red armor, strong melee theme that suits the Indomitus models
- **Custom Chapter** -- pick your own colors, use any Chapter Tactic
