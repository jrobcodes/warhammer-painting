- **Vigilators** hunt psykers and provide cheap melee threat on objectives

## Matchup Notes vs jrob's Necrons

- **500pt:** Slightly Custodes-favored. Darren's quality advantage (T5/T7 models with invulns) beats Necron numbers at small scale.
- **1000pt:** Balanced. Necrons gain Lokhust Heavy Destroyers (anti-tank) and more characters. Custodes gain more bodies and the Blade Champion. Close games expected.
- **2000pt:** Favors skill. Necrons have the C'tan Void Dragon + Lychguard bomb. Custodes have Trajann + Wardens deathstar and deep-striking Allarus. Both armies have powerful combos — the better player wins.
